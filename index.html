Dark Water — Click to Make Waves

Click or drag: each touch creates an expanding circular wave. The simulation is a discrete 2‑D wave equation (linear superposition), so the ripples obey **Huygens’ principle** — overlapping wavefronts form by the sum of many small wavelets.

Letters are drawn **only** where specular highlights occur, so the poem itself becomes the “glitter” on water.
